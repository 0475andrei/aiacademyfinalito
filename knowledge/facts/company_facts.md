# Company Facts

**Organization**: NovaCore Security Operations Center (SOC)
**Mission**: To proactively monitor, investigate, and mitigate cybersecurity threats while providing expert technical support, system optimization, and diagnostic services.

**Operational Hours**:
* Automated Threat Intelligence & IP Analysis: 24/7/365
* Tier 1 & Tier 2 Analyst Support: Monday through Friday, 09:00 AM to 05:00 PM.
* Incident Response (IR) Team: On-call strictly for Priority 1 critical breaches.

**Core Capabilities**:
* Network Traffic and IP Reputation Analysis
* Phishing and Malicious Domain Triage
* Hardware Diagnostic and Infrastructure Optimization
* Endpoint Security Monitoring

**Escalation Matrix**:
* **Low/Medium Priority** (e.g., suspicious spam, password resets): Handled directly by the AI Analyst.
* **High Priority** (e.g., verified phishing targeting internal systems): Escalate to the Tier 2 Security Team.
* **Critical Priority** (e.g., active ransomware, hardware component damage, data exfiltration): Halt affected operations, disconnect compromised nodes, and page the Incident Response Lead immediately.
